Chat Status What Each Status Means

Robylon Platform
Robylon 3 Chat Statuses
🖥 Robylon UI — Chat Status Dropdown

The status dropdown is available at the top of every chat. Tap the current status to change it to Open, Waiting On Agent, or Closed.

On Robylon, every chat has one of three statuses. By default, all newly assigned chats will have an Open status. You must update the status based on the situation as the conversation progresses.

📋 The Three Chat Statuses
Open Default status for all active chats. A chat is Open when you are actively handling it and the conversation is in progress. Keep it Open until there is a specific reason to change it.
Waiting on Agent Use this whenever something is pending whether it is on our side (researching, checking with team, buying time) OR on the customer's side (waiting for them to share a screenshot, document, or reply). This is the single "on hold" status for both scenarios.
Closed Use when: (a) the issue is fully resolved and the customer is satisfied, OR (b) closing after no response following your follow-up, OR (c) closing a junk chat. All chats end as Closed.
💬 Quick Guide — When to Set Each Status
Set Waiting on Agent when… You are checking details or waiting for a response from our team OR you have asked the customer to share something (screenshot, transaction ID, bank statement) and are waiting for their reply. Also set this before logging off for the day.
Set Closed when… Issue is solved and confirmed by customer, OR no response after follow-up (add No Response tag), OR junk chat (add Junk tag). All closures are set to Closed.
⚠ Important
Do not leave chats on Open unnecessarily when something is pending. Update to Waiting on Agent as soon as there is a hold whether it is on your side or the customer's side.

Chat Opening First Thing to Check

As soon as a chat is assigned, check whether the customer has already shared a message or not.

⏳ Scenario A

No message yet — "Hi", "Hello", or blank

  • 1Send a warm greeting within 20–30 minutes.
  • 2Wait for the customer to share their query.
  • 3If no reply, follow the Silence Handling steps.
Sample Greeting

Hi! I am Manorathi. Hope you are doing well! May I know your query, please?

✅ Scenario B

Customer has already shared a query

  • 1Send your greeting first as a separate message.
  • 2Then follow up with acknowledgement or answer in a second message.
  • 3Do NOT ignore the query. Proceed to Step 3 Active Handling.
Sample Greeting then Acknowledgement

Hi! I am Manorathi. Hope you are doing well!

I have received your query regarding [topic]. Please allow me a moment to check this for you.

⚠ Important
Always send your greeting as a separate message first. Then send the acknowledgement or answer as a second message. Do not combine both into one message even if you already know the answer.

Handling Customer Silence (No Response)

This applies when the customer does not reply after your first greeting, and when they go silent mid-chat. Follow the same steps in both cases.

⏱
Wait 20–30 mins after your last message Do not rush. Give the customer enough time to come back and respond.
💬
Send a personalised follow-up message Always reference the customer's topic. Do not send a generic copy-paste message.
Sample Follow-Up (initial silence)

Sir/Madam, are we still connected? The chat is still open with me. Please let me know your query whenever you are ready.

Sample Follow-Up (mid-chat silence)

I hope the above message answered your query regarding [topic]. Please let me know if you have any further questions — I will be happy to assist you.

⚠ Important
If the system has already sent an automated follow-up (after 40–45 minutes), do NOT send another manual follow-up. This is a process deviation.
⏱
Wait another 30 mins after the follow-up Allow time after the follow-up before closing.

Active Query Handling Responding to the User

Once the user shares their query, handle it carefully in the following sequence.

  • 📥
    Read the full query carefully before replying.
    Understand exactly what the user is asking. Do not jump to a reply without reading the full message.
  • 🔍
    Do you already know the answer?
    If yes send your greeting first, then answer in a separate message. Ensure it is accurate and personalised.
    If no send a personalised acknowledgement and set the chat to Waiting on Agent.
    Acknowledgement — Checking Details

    Sir, I have read your query regarding referral benefits. Please allow me some time to check your details and get back to you.

    Acknowledgement — Checking with Team

    I have received your query about repayment tracking. Please allow me some time to check with my team and respond.

    Set status: Waiting on Agent
    🚨 Process Breach
    Failing to set the chat to Waiting on Agent after sending an acknowledgement is a process breach.
  • 📎
    Need something from the customer?
    If your answer depends on information from the user such as a screenshot, or document ask for it and set the chat to Waiting on Agent.
    Sample — Requesting Information from Customer

    Sir, to help you with this, could you please share a screenshot of the page you got stuck? This will help me check this for you at the earliest.

    Set status: Waiting on Agent
    💡 Note
    Waiting on Agent is used for both when WE are researching and when the CUSTOMER needs to share something. It is the single hold status for all pending situations.
  • ✍️
    Draft your final response.
    Use the Writer tool to draft and refine. Cover all queries, use the customer's wording, break into points, include links or screenshots, and ensure accuracy. If unsure consult SME or TL. Do not guess.
  • 👀
    Re-read the full chat before sending.
    Check that no question has been missed. Ensure the answer is complete and personalised.
  • 📤
    Send the final answer within 1 hour of acknowledgement.
    Copy the finalised draft and send. Do not combine the final answer with a follow-up message.
  • 🔁
    Customer has another question?
    Continue from the top. Keep handling until all queries are fully resolved.

Personalisation Quick Check Before Every Message Ask Yourself:

✅ Have I answered everything?
✅ Is this personalised?
✅ Is this technically correct?

Junk Chat Handling

A chat is considered junk in any of the following situations. Handle it politely and close as Closed with the Junk tag.

  • 🔗Customer sends an unknown link, suspicious image, or completely irrelevant content.
  • ✓Customer says their issue was already resolved before the conversation started.
  • ❓Message is random and has no relation to any service we offer.
Send closure message and close as Closed with Junk tag

Resolution Status — Resolved Toggle (Right Sidebar)

Set Before Closing Every Chat

In addition to setting the Chat Status to Closed, you must also update the Resolved property in the Properties panel on the right sidebar before closing any chat. This is a separate mandatory step — it is not the same as changing the chat status.

🖥 Robylon UI — Properties Panel (Right Sidebar)

The Resolved dropdown is in the Properties panel on the right sidebar. Set it to Yes or No based on whether the query was actually resolved, then click Save.

✅ Set Resolved → YES when…
Query is fully resolved Customer confirmed the answer, said "Thank you", "Okay", or "Got it". The issue is genuinely sorted and the customer is satisfied.
Call resolved the query A call was connected, the query was answered on the call, and the post-call summary has been sent on chat.
❌ Set Resolved → NO when…
No response from customer Customer did not reply after your follow-up. Chat is being closed due to silence — the query was not confirmed as resolved by the customer.
Junk chat Irrelevant message, unknown link, or already resolved before chat started. No actual query was handled in this conversation.
Call not answered You attempted the call but it went unanswered. The customer did not connect and no resolution was provided.
⚠ Two Separate Steps to Close a Chat on Robylon
Closing a chat correctly requires two separate actions:
Step 1: Set the Chat Status to Closed using the dropdown at the top of the chat window.
Step 2: Set Resolved → Yes or No in the Properties panel on the right sidebar, then click Save.
Do not skip either step.

Shift Ending Protocol

If your shift is about to end and there are open chats, follow these steps in exact order. Never log off without completing all steps.

1
Acknowledge the last customer message in each open chat.
2
Send a personalised logging-off message to the customer.
3
Set the chat status to Waiting on Agent.
4
Change your Agent Availability to Unavailable (Offline).
Sample Logging-Off Message

I am logging off for the day, but your chat is still open. I will update you as soon as I receive a response from the concerned team. If you have any questions, feel free to drop them here. We will respond when we log in tomorrow at 10 AM. Thank you for your understanding. Have a good night!

⚠ Order Matters
Change availability to Unavailable only after all log-off messages are sent and all open chats are set to Waiting on Agent. Do not go Unavailable before completing these steps.

Communication Standards

These standards apply to every single message you send. There are no exceptions.

✓ Always Do This

  • Use simple, clear language — avoid jargon
  • Use proper grammar and punctuation in every message
  • Break long answers into smaller, easy-to-read points
  • Use words like "Apologies" and "Thank you" appropriately
  • Wish the customer on festivals and weekends when relevant
  • Appreciate the user when they share requested details
  • Include the user's query wording in your response
  • Consult SME or TL when unsure of the answer

✕ Never Do This

  • Use words like suggestion, recommendation, advice
  • Ask for bank statements or CMR documents over chat
  • Provide incomplete or partial answers
  • Ignore any query — even a minor one
  • Send copy-paste robotic responses
  • Send one-word replies
  • Guess an answer you are not sure about
  • Rush to close a chat before all queries are resolved

Personalisation Example

Instead of a generic closing like this:

"I hope I was able to address all your queries with the solutions provided above."

💡 Educational Responsibility
Your role is to educate users not advise them. Clarify doubts, explain processes, and guide them through platform usage. Give the right amount of information do not overwhelm.
